[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Pouring out my glass, I don't need it tonight
Your honey-sweet lips are my only vice
We're locking the door, letting go of the time
Giving this whiskey river a sweet goodbye

[Verse 1]
The neon sign is buzzing out on the street
But in this candlelit corner, it's just you and me
You trace a little circle on the edge of my hand
Pulling me closer, making me your man

[Pre-Chorus]
Shadows are dancing across your skin
I'm feeling the heat of where we begin
No rush at all, we're taking it slow
I want every part of you, body and soul

[Chorus]
Oh, the whiskey river is running dry
But I'm drowning deep in your midnight eyes
Wrap your arms around me, pull me into the fire
Tonight we're riding on a slow-burn desire

[Post-Chorus]
Yeah, we're saying goodbye to the lonely nights
Just you and me in the fading light

[Verse 2]
I hear the rain falling soft on the tin
But the storm in my chest is about to begin
You whisper a secret right into my ear
Everything else just disappears

We're lost in a rhythm away from the crowd
[Whispered] (Say my name softly... Saeed tonight...)
